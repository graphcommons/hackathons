Graph Commons (https://graphcommons.com) is a collaborative platform for mapping, analyzing and publishing data-networks. It empowers people and organizations to transform their data into interactive maps and untangle complex relations that impact them and their communities.

Graph Commons members have been using the platform for the following purposes:

  1. Community Strategy (managing and optimizing relationships, e.g., ecosystem mapping, community management, organizational analysis, contact mapping, partnership strategy...)
  2. Innovation Strategy (discovering opportunities, revealing invisible patterns e.g., issue/concept mapping, knowledge graphs, investigative journalism, tech scouting, investment strategy, market analysis, content strategy, policy mapping, blockchain analysis...)
